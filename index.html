HOW TO PLAY

1.Click on “Count Me In” to join the challenge. Your SBOBET account will be registered to the challenge.




2.Bets placed on African Cup of Nation 2019 from the Promotion Period will be counted to the challenge result.

3.SBOBET African Cup Challenge will have 3 Categories consists with 3 Stages:

Categories

a. Net WinLoss – Total Win Amount minus Total Loss Amount

b.Winning Bets – Total Winning Bets Count minus Total Losing Bets Count

c. Winning Percentage – Total Winning Bets Count divide Total Bets Count

Stages

a. Group Stage – start from 25th June 2019 to 2nd July 2019.

b. Knockout Stage – start from 5th July 2019 to 19th July 2019.

c. Ultimate Stage – 25th June 2019 to 19th July 2019 (combination results of Group and Knockout Stage)

Requirement to participate the challenge

a. Minimum Stake per bet: IDR100k

b. Winning Percentage: Bet on 15 different matches of African Cup of Nation 2019
